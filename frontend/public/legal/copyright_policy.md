# Copyright Policy

**Effective Date:** June 26, 2026

**The Helping Society** (the "Platform") respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects its users to do the same. This Copyright Policy outlines the rules regarding content uploads and the procedure to request the removal of infringing materials.

As an independent, student-led college community initiative for Ujjain Engineering College (UECU), we take copyright compliance seriously.

---

## 1. User Upload Obligations & Ownership

When you upload academic resources (notes, PYQs, study guides, slides, or books) or post listings (Marketplace, Rooms, Lost & Found, Opportunities) on this Platform, you agree to the following terms:
* **Ownership:** Users retain ownership of the content they upload.
* **User Responsibility:** Users are solely responsible for ensuring they possess the necessary rights, licenses, or permissions before uploading any content.
* **Allowed Sharing:** You represent and warrant that you are the author/creator of the content, or have obtained explicit permission or a license from the copyright owner, or that the sharing constitutes "fair deal" or "fair use" under applicable copyright laws (such as Section 52 of the Indian Copyright Act, 1957, which permits the use of copyrighted works for private study, research, or criticism).
* **Restrictions:** Do not upload scanned textbooks, published test prep manuals, or proprietary classroom materials if you do not have permission from the publisher or professor. This obligation applies to all Students, Faculty, Alumni, and Contributors.

---

## 2. Copyright Infringement Takedown Procedure

Copyright holders may request the removal of infringing material. If you are a copyright owner, publisher, or authorized representative and believe that any material hosted on our Platform infringes your copyright, you may submit a takedown request.

Please send a written notification to our support team at **support@thehelpingsociety.in** containing the following details:
1. **Identification of the Work:** A clear description of the copyrighted work that you claim has been infringed (e.g., textbook title, specific lecture slides, etc.).
2. **Location on the Platform:** The specific URL, resource title, or identifier where the alleged infringing material is located on the Platform, so we can locate it.
3. **Contact Information:** Your full name, physical address, telephone number, and email address.
4. **Statement of Good Faith:** A statement by you that you have a good faith belief that the disputed use of the material is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
5. **Statement of Accuracy:** A statement by you, under penalty of perjury, that the information in your notice is accurate and that you are the copyright owner or authorized to act on the copyright owner's behalf.
6. **Signature:** Your physical or electronic signature.

---

## 3. Administrative Action & Enforcement

Upon receiving a valid copyright notice:
* **Immediate Removal:** Platform Administrators may review the notice and remove or disable access to the infringing material **without prior notice**.
* **Uploader Notification:** The user who uploaded the material will be notified of the removal.
* **Repeat Infringer Policy:** To protect intellectual property rights, the Platform maintains a strict policy regarding repeat offenders. Users who repeatedly upload copyrighted materials without authorization will have their accounts temporarily disabled or permanently banned.
