# Cookie Policy

**Effective Date:** June 26, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how **The Helping Society** (the "Platform") uses cookies and similar technologies to manage sessions, authenticate users, and secure our services. 

As an independent community platform for Ujjain Engineering College (UECU), we believe in user privacy. We do not use cookies for tracking, behavioral profiling, or advertising. 

---

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by your web browser when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide security.

---

## 2. Essential and Functional Cookies

The Platform uses functional authentication and session cookies, which are strictly required to verify identity and maintain active sessions:

### Session and Authentication Cookies
* **Type:** Essential / Functional Cookies
* **Provider:** First-party (set by the Platform's servers)
* **Lifespan:** Stored on your device for limited durations (up to the platform-designated period) to keep you logged in.
* **Security Settings:**
  * **HttpOnly:** These cookies cannot be accessed or read by client-side scripts. This prevents session data theft via Cross-Site Scripting (XSS) attacks.
  * **Secure:** Transmitted strictly over encrypted connections (HTTPS).
  * **SameSite=Strict:** Ensures session cookies are only sent to our server during direct navigation from our site. This prevents Cross-Site Request Forgery (CSRF) attacks.
* **Purpose:** These cookies store secure session tokens. They enable session rotation and security detection to mitigate session hijacking. If unauthorized reuse is detected by the server, these cookies are immediately cleared.

Without these cookies, you would be forced to log in again every time you navigate to a new page or refresh your browser.

## 3. Analytics and Marketing Cookies

* **No Marketing Cookies:** We do not display ads and do not use marketing, targeting, or advertising cookies.
* **No Third-Party Trackers:** We do not load external tracker scripts that track your behavior across other sites.
* **Future Analytics:** If we implement lightweight, privacy-focused analytics in the future to monitor platform uptime and page performance, we will update this policy and seek user consent where required by applicable laws.

---

## 4. How to Manage Cookies

Since authentication cookies are essential for the security and operation of the Platform, you cannot opt out of them while logged in. If you block these cookies via your browser settings, you will not be able to log in or access any authenticated features.

If you wish to remove these cookies:
1. Log out of the Platform. Logging out calls the server to clear the authentication cookies from your browser automatically.
2. Alternatively, you can clear your browser's cookies and site data for the Platform at any time through your browser's settings:
   * **Chrome:** Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data.
   * **Firefox:** Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data > Manage Data.
   * **Safari:** Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data.
