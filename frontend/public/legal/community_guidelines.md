# Community Guidelines

**Effective Date:** June 26, 2026

Welcome to **The Helping Society** community! This Platform is built to foster collaboration, academic success, and mutual support among Students, Faculty, Alumni, and verified Contributors of **Ujjain Engineering College (UECU)**.

As an independent, student-led college community initiative, we rely on our members to maintain a safe, clean, and productive digital space. To ensure this, all registered users must adhere to these Community Guidelines.

---

## 1. Core Principles

* **Respect and Civility:** Treat all members with respect. Discussions should be constructive. Personal attacks, insults, harassment, or aggressive behavior will not be tolerated. This applies to all Students, Faculty, Alumni, Contributors, and Admins.
* **Academic Integrity:** Share only study materials, notes, and resources that help others learn legitimately. Do not use the Platform to share exam solutions, cheat sheets during ongoing tests, or promote academic dishonesty.
* **Community Responsibility:** If you see content that violates these guidelines, report it immediately to the Administrators.

---

## 2. Prohibited Conduct

Any user who participates in the following activities will face disciplinary action, up to a permanent account ban:

### A. Harassment, Discrimination, and Bullying
* **No Harassment:** Do not harass, stalk, threaten, or intimidate other Students, Faculty, Alumni, or Contributors.
* **Zero Tolerance for Hate Speech:** Do not post content that promotes hatred, discrimination, or violence against individuals or groups based on academic branch, registration year, race, religion, gender, sexual orientation, or disability.

### B. Spam, Impersonation, and Misrepresentation
* **No Spam or Self-Promotion:** Do not flood comments, opportunities, or community posts with unsolicited advertisements, promotional links, or repetitive messages.
* **No Impersonation:** Do not represent yourself as someone else. It is strictly prohibited to impersonate UECU Faculty, Admins, other Students, Alumni, or organization representatives.
* **No Fake Opportunities:** Contributors and Admins must post only legitimate jobs, internships, workshops, or mentorship programs. Posting fake listings, misleading URLs, or multi-level marketing (MLM) schemes is forbidden.

### C. Illegal Listings and Misuse of Modules
* **No Illegal Marketplace Listings:** Items listed on the Marketplace must comply with local laws and college regulations. Do not list drugs, alcohol, weapons, hazardous materials, counterfeit items, or academic cheating services.
* **No Misleading Support Requests:** The Support Module is intended for academic support, general inquiries, and real-life emergencies. Submitting false support tickets, prank emergency requests, or misleading descriptions is a severe violation.

### D. Copyright and Intellectual Property Violation
* **No Unauthorized Sharing:** Do not upload resources (books, study packs, test keys) that violate copyrights. Only upload content that you personally created, or that is public domain, or for which you have explicit permission to distribute.

---

## 3. Commenting and Discussion Rules

To keep the Platform readable and safe:
* **Plain Text Only:** All comments and replies must be plain text. HTML tags are strictly blocked to prevent security vulnerabilities.
* **No Flame Wars:** Do not engage in arguments or hostile threads. Comments are restricted to a limited nested depth to keep discussions focused and organized.

---

## 4. Disclaimers

### A. Career Opportunities Disclaimer
Publication of opportunities on the Platform does NOT constitute endorsement, verification, recommendation, or guarantee of authenticity by the Platform. Users are solely responsible for independently verifying opportunities before applying or sharing personal information.

### B. Artificial Intelligence Disclaimer
Some descriptions, recommendations, summaries, or future platform features may use Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies. Users should independently verify important information before relying on it.

---

## 5. Reporting Abuse and Violations

If you encounter posts, opportunities, resources, or comments that violate these guidelines:
1. Use the **Report** button/icon next to the content.
2. Provide a brief reason for the report.
3. Reports are sent directly to the Admin Dashboard for review.

---

## 6. Consequences of Violations

Administrators review all reports and audit logs. The actions taken depend on the severity of the violation:
1. **Content Removal:** Any violating resource, opportunity, community post, or comment will be immediately deleted.
2. **Account Disabling:** For minor infractions, an account may be temporarily disabled. The user will be blocked from logging in until they contact the admin.
3. **Permanent Ban:** For severe violations (e.g., harassment, fake emergency requests, illegal marketplace listings, repeat copyright infringement), the user will be permanently banned. Banned users are blocked from logging in, their access is revoked, and they will receive a notification email explaining the ban.

---

## 7. Future Features and Policy Updates

The Platform may introduce new features, services, authentication methods, communication channels, integrations, and moderation capabilities over time. These guidelines may be updated accordingly.
