# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 26, 2026

Welcome to **The Helping Society** (referred to as the "Platform," "we," "us," or "our"), an independent college community platform built for students, faculty, alumni, contributors, and administrators of **Ujjain Engineering College (UECU)**.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your personal information when you register, log in, or interact with our website and services.

---

### Important Notice: Independent Community Initiative
The Helping Society is an independent community initiative developed by and for UECU community members. Unless explicitly stated, this Platform is **not an official website of Ujjain Engineering College**, and the content posted by users represents their own individual views, not those of the institution.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We only collect information necessary to verify your affiliation with the college community and to provide our community features. The details we collect depend on your account type:

### A. Account Registration Information
* **Students:** 
  * Full Name
  * Registration Number (validated against college enrollment patterns)
  * Academic Branch
  * Year of Registration
  * Date of Birth (used for identity verification)
  * Phone Number
  * College-issued Email Address (verified to confirm active enrollment)
  * Secure Password
* **Faculty:**
  * Full Name
  * Professional Email Address
  * Phone Number
  * Secure Password
* **Alumni:**
  * Full Name
  * Personal or Professional Email Address
  * Phone Number
  * Academic Branch
  * Year of Graduation
  * Current Company and Role
  * LinkedIn Profile Link (optional)
  * Secure Password
* **Contributors (Recruiters, External Mentors, Organizations):**
  * Full Name
  * Work Email Address
  * Organization Name
  * Role or Position in the Organization
  * Secure Password
* **Administrators:**
  * Full Name
  * Administrator Email Address
  * Secure Password

### B. User-Generated Content and Uploads
We store the files, images, and text you upload to enable core platform features:
* **Academic Resources:** Study materials, notes, past year question papers (PYQs), books, and syllabus files (subject to platform-specified file type and size limits).
* **Community Posts:**
  * **Lost & Found:** Titles, descriptions, contact numbers, locations, and images.
  * **Rooms:** Titles, descriptions, rent pricing, locations, contact numbers, and images.
  * **Marketplace:** Titles, descriptions, pricing, contact numbers, and images (up to the platform-designated limit).
* **Support & Emergency Requests:** Support descriptions (subject to platform-designated length limits), contact numbers, locations, and images. You may choose to submit these requests anonymously.
* **Comments and Replies:** Plain text comments and replies (subject to length limits; code-injected tags and HTML are blocked for safety).

## 2. Technical Data, Cookies, and Session Authentication

To ensure platform security and keep you logged in, we collect technical session data:
* **Session Authentication:** We use secure session authentication technologies to authorize requests.
* **Authentication Cookies:** We set essential, secure session cookies in your browser. These cookies are configured with strict security headers (preventing access by client-side scripts, restricted to encrypted traffic, and restricted to first-party requests) to safeguard against cross-site security threats.
* **Security & Session Management:** We use token rotation and security detection to mitigate session hijacking. If unauthorized reuse is detected, session cookies are cleared automatically.
* **No Marketing or Tracking Cookies:** We do not use advertising, marketing, or behavioral tracking cookies, nor do we share your browsing activity with third-party advertising networks.

---

## 3. Storage and Third-Party Infrastructure

Your information is processed and stored securely using the following third-party infrastructure:
* **Database Infrastructure:** All database records, user profiles, posts, comments, and metadata are stored on industry-standard database systems.
* **Cloud Storage Providers:** Uploaded files (academic resources) and images (Marketplace, Rooms, Lost & Found, Support Requests) are hosted securely with cloud storage providers, which generate encrypted access links.
* **Email Communication Services:** Automated transactional emails (verification links, password resets, and account status notifications) are dispatched through secure email delivery providers.

---

## 4. Data Retention and Anonymization

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy:
* **Operational Necessity:** Active accounts, posts, and resources are retained to support community interactions and allow users to access files.
* **Audit and Moderation Logs:** To maintain platform integrity and security, administrative actions (approvals, rejections, bans, role changes) are recorded in temporary audit logs that are automatically purged after a short administrative cycle.
* **Time-to-Live (TTL) Content:** Certain community listings, such as resolved Lost & Found posts and temporary room postings, are automatically deleted from our databases after short, predefined operational timelines.
* **Anonymization:** Upon account deletion, comments and forum discussions may be anonymized (removing the author's name and linking the post to a generic "Deleted User" label) rather than completely deleted to preserve thread continuity for other community members. Contributed academic resources may also be anonymized and kept available to avoid academic disruption for current students.

## 5. User Rights and Data Management

We respect your rights regarding your personal data. Depending on your role and applicable regulations in India, you have the following rights:
* **Access and Portability:** You may view the personal data associated with your account in your profile dashboard. Where applicable and technically feasible, you may request a copy of your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format.
* **Data Correction:** You may edit your profile information or update details directly through your account settings.
* **Account Deletion:** You may request complete deletion of your account. Upon verification, your personal profile data and credentials will be removed.
* **Data Deletion Exception Policy:** Please note that certain records may be retained post-deletion for security, fraud prevention, moderation, audit logging, dispute resolution, or to comply with legal obligations.
* **How to Exercise Rights:** To exercise any of these rights, please contact the Platform Administrators at [support@thehelpingsociety.in](mailto:support@thehelpingsociety.in).

## 6. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes the use of encryption during data transmission, secure password hashing algorithms, strict role-based access control, and firewalls on our database servers. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The Platform is intended exclusively for college students, faculty, alumni, and professional contributors. It is not directed at, nor do we knowingly collect personal data from, children under the age of 13 (or under the age of majority in your jurisdiction). If we discover that a child has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.
