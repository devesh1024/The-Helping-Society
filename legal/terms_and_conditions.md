* **Submission Workflow:** Students and Core Team members can submit resource upload requests (notes, PYQs, study guides). These resources are queued and will only go live after **Admin Approval**. Faculty, Alumni, and Administrators can view resources. Faculty and Administrators can upload resources directly. All files are subject to platform-specified type and size limits.

Users are solely responsible for independently verifying opportunities before applying, attending events, or sharing personal information.
