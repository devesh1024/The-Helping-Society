# Safety Guidelines

**Effective Date:** June 26, 2026

Welcome to **The Helping Society**! To ensure that our campus community remains a secure and trusted environment, we have put together these educational Safety Guidelines. 

Whether you are buying textbooks, renting a room, checking a job opportunity, or responding to a support request, following these precautions will help keep you and your fellow UECU peers safe.

---

## 1. Account Security

Protecting your account credentials is the first line of defense:
* **Never Share Passwords:** Keep your account password confidential. Platform Administrators will never ask for your password.
* **Never Share OTPs (One-Time Passwords):** If the Platform or any connected email/service sends you a verification link or a security code, do not share it with anyone.
* **Use Strong Passwords:** Use a unique password that you do not reuse on other websites.

---

## 2. In-Person Meeting Safety (Marketplace & Room Listings)

When buying or selling items via the Marketplace, or visiting accommodations listed in the Rooms board:
* **Meet in Public Places:** Always arrange to meet in well-lit, highly populated public areas. For UECU students, meeting on-campus (e.g., library lobbies, college canteen, main gate) during daytime hours is strongly recommended.
* **Never Go Alone:** If you are visiting a room listing or meeting a buyer/seller for a high-value item, bring a friend or classmate with you.
* **Verify Identities:** Confirm that the person you are meeting is indeed a registered student, alumnus, or faculty member of the UECU community before completing any exchange.

---

## 3. Financial Precautions

* **Avoid Advance Payments:** Never wire money, transfer funds via UPI, or pay in advance to a seller you do not know. Only hand over payment once you have met the seller, inspected the item, and are fully satisfied with its condition.
* **Inspect Items Thoroughly:** Before buying electronics, books, or cycles, check their condition to ensure they match the description and function properly.
* **Exercise Caution in Room Bookings:** Do not pay security deposits or rent advances before physically inspecting the room and verifying the lease details.

---

## 4. Opportunity & Support Requests Verification

* **Verify Opportunities Independently:** Before applying to jobs or internships listed in the Opportunities board, research the organization. Do not share sensitive details like your Aadhaar number, PAN card, or bank account details with recruiters unless you have verified their legitimacy.
* **Be Cautious with Support Requests:** When responding to support requests (especially those submitted anonymously), verify the requester's identity if possible. For emergency requests, ensure that meeting places are safe and public.
* **Never download files from users outside the Platform or from unknown shortened URLs.**
---

## 5. Reporting and Active Community Protection

Maintaining a safe community is a shared responsibility. If you notice any suspicious activity, please report it:
* **Report Suspicious Users:** If a user behaves suspiciously, insists on advance payments, or refuses to meet in public, report them using the **Report** button.
* **Report Fake Opportunities:** If an opportunity listing contains broken links, requests payment to apply, or seems too good to be true, flag it immediately.
* **Report Abusive Behaviour:** Report any harassment, abusive language, or spam.
* **Contact Support:** You can reach the Platform Administrators directly by email at [support@thehelpingsociety.in](mailto:support@thehelpingsociety.in).
